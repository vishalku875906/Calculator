Overview

This Mortgage Pro Calculator helps you estimate monthly payments for home loans, view a full amortization schedule, and understand the interest vs principal breakdown over the life of the loan. It is designed for borrowers, real-estate professionals and financial planners who need accurate and shareable schedules including CSV export and print-ready results.

How it works

The calculator uses the standard annuity formula to compute the fixed monthly payment:

Monthly payment = P × r / (1 − (1 + r)⁻ⁿ), where P = principal, r = monthly interest rate, n = total number of payments.

We compute each month’s interest as the current balance × monthly rate, subtract interest from payment to get principal repaid, then reduce the balance. The amortization table clearly shows these values so you can plan extra payments or compare loan options.

Example

For a ₹50,00,000 loan at 7.5% annual interest for 20 years, the calculator shows the monthly payment, total interest paid, and the month-by-month amortization. Use the Export CSV button to download the schedule and analyze it in a spreadsheet.

Tips to reduce total interest

  • Make extra monthly or annual payments — even small amounts reduce interest and shorten the loan term.
  • Refinance to a lower rate if rates drop and refinancing costs are justified.
  • Choose shorter terms where affordable — a 15-year loan often saves substantial interest compared to a 30-year loan.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I include extra payments?

Yes — you can plan extra monthly payments and the amortization schedule will reflect faster principal reduction.

Is this calculation accurate for all loan types?

It works for fixed-rate annuity loans. For interest-only, adjustable-rate, or balloon loans consult a loan officer or use specialized calculators.

How does CSV export work?

CSV exports every row of the amortization table (month, payment, principal, interest, balance) for further analysis in Excel or Google Sheets.
