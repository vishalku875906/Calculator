About EMI Pro

The EMI Pro Calculator helps you plan loan repayments with precision. It supports additional monthly payments, exports amortization schedules to CSV, and provides print-friendly reports. Use it to compare different loan terms, simulate extra payments, and measure the savings from accelerated repayment.

Calculation details

The calculator uses the fixed-payment formula for amortizing loans. When you add an extra monthly payment, the calculator applies it directly to principal after each payment, which reduces future interest and shortens the term. The amortization table is recalculated to reflect these changes.

Use case example

Loan: ₹10,00,000 | Rate: 8% annual | Term: 10 years. Baseline EMI shows the monthly cost and total interest. If you add ₹2,000 extra per month, you can see the new payoff date and interest savings immediately.

Best practices

  • Compare multiple loan amounts and terms with the same tool to find an affordable monthly payment.
  • Model one-time lump-sum pre-payments to see their effect on remaining balance.
  • Download the CSV to keep records or share with advisors.

FAQ

Will extra payments always shorten the loan?

Yes — additional principal payments reduce outstanding balance and therefore future interest; pay attention to prepayment penalties on your loan contract.

Does this handle variable interest rates?

No — this tool assumes a fixed interest rate for the modeled period. For adjustable-rate loans use a dedicated ARM calculator.
